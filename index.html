About

After graduating from university in 2010, I worked in the charitable sector for a short period before arriving at the Royal College of Veterinary Surgeons. At the College I was involved with the relaunch of the College's Practice Standards Scheme, accrediting veterinary practices, and the development of an online platform to help practices achieve accreditation. I left the RCVS in the summer of 2017 to pursue a career in web development. My first step is starting the General Assembly Immersive Web Development course in August 2017.
