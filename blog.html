15 August 2017

Today was lots of work on HTML. While most of it was going over things I was pretty familiar with already, the details on forms were interesting and new. Something I look to implement on this website, as part of the contact section as soon as I learn to do anything more than have them just collect information.

Additionally I learnt about the three excellent things. The first two, FillMurray and PlaceCage are both fun and I'm sure will be useful in the future. The bigger a-ha moment was the realisation I should have been making my small CSS tweaks in my browser's dev tools. I felt like a fool thinking of the huge amount of time I've spent making things looking just right, flicking between a style sheet and refreshing a browser. No more. Happy day.
